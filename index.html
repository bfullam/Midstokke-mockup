Aerospace

A complete line of o-rings, seals and custom gaskets for the aerospace industry. We are suppliers of Boeing and Airbus approved o-rings and seals.

Automotive

A full line of automotive seals and custom gaskets to the automotive industry. Our products feature full PPAP capability.

Petroleum

A complete line of oil seals and oversized custom o-rings that meet petroleum and oil exploration industry standards.

Medical

An extensive line of FDA conforming o-rings and seals, as well as medical grade o-rings and seals.

Military

Our complete line of military grade Mil-Spec o-rings, seals and custom gaskets can include AS9102 First Articles and samples, per customer specifications.
